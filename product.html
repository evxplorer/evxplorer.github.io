Our Solutions

Tailor-Made solutions in various domains.

Route Optimization

This solution is for all EV users. The objective is to identify the optimum route and provide navigation assistance starting from the source upto the destination. The optimum route depends on various constraints including time, distance, charging requirement, travel cost, etc.

EV charging station placement

This solution is specifically for EV manufacturers and EV infrastructure developers. The existence of public charging infrastructure boosts the early adoption of EVs. But due to lack of vacant lands and varying charging requirements across the region, Charging infrastructure can't be developed everywhere. Based on the available realtime data of EV locations, Infrastructure development cost, government initiatives and electric grid constraints, optimal locations for developing Charging Stations needs to be identified.

Charge Scheduling

Since the Charging Stations receive a number of prior charging requests whilst it can serve only a limited number of users, a proper scheduling algorithm needs to be in place that not only mimimizes the service denials but also takes into account user availability time window, and other constraints. This solution thus serves both charging stations and EV users.

Battery Management System

In an EV, the most important and expensive part is the battery of the vehicle. Thus it needs to be managed properly so as to increase its life and performance. This product thus serves EV users.

Logistics Optimization

The contribution of EVs in logistics is quiet less in India due to several charging constraints and limited range. Inorder to make logistics via EVs smooth, such solutions are required. This product thus relates to business logistics.
