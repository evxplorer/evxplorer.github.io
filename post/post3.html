Ministry of Heavy industries has sanctioned around 520 Charging Stations under the Phase-I of FAME-India Scheme, 2877 Charging Stations in 68 cities and 1576 Charging Stations across 9 Expressways and 16 Highways across 25 States/UTs in phase-II of FAME-India Scheme. Out of 520 charging stations sanctioned under Phase-I, 452 EV charging stations have already been installed. In Phase-II of FAME-India Scheme, Rs. 1000 Cr. is allocated for development of charging infrastructure. This Ministry has sanctioned 2877 Electric Vehicle Charging Stations in 68 cities across 25 States/UTs. Further, 1576 charging stations have been sanctioned across 9 Expressways and 16 Highways under Phase II of FAME India Scheme.
