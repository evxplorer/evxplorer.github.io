    Our Humble Beginnings

    Established as a Company

    Our company was established in 2021, with an objective of identifying and finding solutions for EV related problems and thus diversifying the use of Green vehicles. While we are still rooted to our core-values, we always have a forward looking approach.

    Our Story

    EVXplorer is a budding organization based in Patna, India addressing the problem of adapting EVs in India. We are a trusted development partner for developing applications and algorithms for EV users and Infrastructures. Our talent management and development processes are geared to meet the needs of rapidly evolving products and dynamic development cycles.
